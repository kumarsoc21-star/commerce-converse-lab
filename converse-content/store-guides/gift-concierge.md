# Gift Concierge Services

## Wrapping
Physical Apple and Samsung boxes can ship in a **Trail & Ember gift box** for $4.99. Digital gift cards include a printable PDF card with your message.

## Message cards
Free printed card with physical orders up to **250 characters**. Digital orders include email gift note text.

## Gift receipts
Recipients exchange physical items with a gift receipt code. Digital gift cards transfer by forwarding the email — treat codes like cash once sent.

## Ebook gifting
Ebook and audiobook gifts send a redemption link. The recipient chooses their reader app where supported.

## Digital gift cards
Apple, Samsung, Amazon, Spotify, Netflix, Uber, and Trail & Ember store credit deliver within minutes. Never expire unless noted on the card page.

## Curated suggestions
Ask for "Apple and Samsung gifts under $50" — the assistant may recommend cases, chargers, and digital cards; each line is added separately after you confirm.
