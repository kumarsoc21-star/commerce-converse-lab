# Cart and Order Amendments

## While status is Processing
Change shipping address, add a line, or cancel for a full refund **before** the order leaves Processing. Tell Converse your order number and the change. Once status is Shipped, use returns instead.

## Cancelling
Processing orders cancel immediately with email confirmation. Shipped orders must be refused at delivery or returned under the Trailhead Returns Program.

## Out of stock after checkout
If inventory drops after you pay, we email within 24 hours with substitute, backorder, or partial refund options. Other lines ship on schedule.

## Combining shipments
We cannot merge two placed orders. Cancel and reorder while both are still Processing if you need one box.

## Cart vs order
The Converse **cart** is pre-checkout. Use chat or the sidebar to add, remove, or clear lines. **Orders** exist only after checkout on the real storefront; the lab demo stops at cart hold.

## Wrong address
Verify at checkout. Converse can start a carrier trace ticket but cannot reroute packages in the demo environment.
