# Converse Shopper Helpdesk

## What is Converse Commerce?
Converse Commerce is the AI gift assistant for Trail & Ember Gift Co. Ask in plain language for Apple, Samsung, ebooks, gift cards, accessories, or smart-home ideas. The assistant searches our live catalog and store guides — it does not invent products or policies.

## How do I find a gift under a budget?
Describe who you are shopping for and your limit — for example, "Apple gift for Marcus under $100." The assistant searches Apple, Samsung, Ebooks, Gift Cards, Accessories, and Smart Home departments and shows in-stock product cards.

## How does the cart work?
Add items with **Add to cart** on product cards, or ask the assistant to place a line for you. Cart writes require you to click **Confirm** when prompted — the assistant will not change your cart without that step.

## Can the assistant remove something I added?
Yes. Ask "remove the AirPods from my cart." The assistant reads your cart, identifies the SKU (for example `APPL-001` for AirPods Pro), and requests confirmation before removing it.

## Digital vs physical items
Gift cards and ebooks deliver by email, usually within minutes. Apple and Samsung hardware ships from Ember Lane per our delivery guide.

## What if the assistant cannot answer?
If a store-guide lookup returns no relevant excerpt, the assistant may offer to open a support case. A human replies by email within one business day, Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM ET.

## Demo sessions
The web demo uses shopper ID `demo-shopper`. Your cart persists for that ID until you clear it or use another browser profile.
