# Checkout and Wallet

## Payment methods
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, and Apple, Samsung, Amazon, and Trail & Ember digital gift cards. One gift card plus one other method per order.

## Charge timing
Cards are authorized at checkout and captured when the order ships. Pre-orders charge only on dispatch.

## Pricing
All amounts are USD. Sales tax is computed from the shipping address at checkout. Promotions do not stack unless the banner says so. Price-drop adjustments within **7 days** are available via support ticket.

## Gift cards
Digital only; never expire; not redeemable for cash except where state law requires.

## Security
PCI-compliant processor; we do not store full card numbers. Converse never asks for payment details in chat.

## Lab demo note
The Converse Commerce Lab web UI does not process real payments. Cart lines are held for practice only; no card is charged.
