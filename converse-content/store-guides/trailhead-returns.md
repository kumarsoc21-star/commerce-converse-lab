# Trailhead Returns Program

## Standard return window
Sealed physical items (Apple, Samsung, accessories, smart-home hardware) may be returned within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and resalable. **Returns at 45 days or later are not accepted** unless the gift-season extension below applies.

## Gift-season extension
Gift orders purchased **May 1 – June 30** receive a **60-day** return window from delivery.

## Digital exceptions
**Gift cards, ebook downloads, and subscription codes** are final sale once the code is emailed or redeemed. Defective unredeemed digital codes may be exchanged within **7 days**.

## Opened electronics
Opened Apple or Samsung devices may incur a restocking fee if not defective. Contact support before returning opened phones, tablets, or watches.

## How to start a return
Tell the Converse assistant you want to return an item and provide your order number. You will receive a prepaid label by email for physical goods; ship within 14 days of label issue.

## Refund timing
Refunds post to the original payment method within **5–7 business days** after we receive and inspect the return.
