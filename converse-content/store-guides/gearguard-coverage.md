# GearGuard Product Coverage

## One-year baseline
Physical Trail & Ember catalog items carry a **1-year limited warranty** against defects from delivery. Manufacturer warranties also apply on Apple and Samsung sealed retail products.

## Brand-specific coverage
**Apple** hardware follows Apple's limited warranty; register at apple.com/support. **Samsung** devices include Samsung's standard U.S. warranty — retain proof of purchase. **Accessories** from third-party brands (Anker, Belkin, Spigen, Logitech) follow each maker's policy listed on the product page.

## Digital goods
Ebooks, subscription codes, and gift cards are **not warrantied** after delivery or redemption. Defective unredeemed codes may be replaced within **7 days** with support ticket proof.

## Not covered
Accidental damage, lost gift codes after email delivery, redeemed ebooks, and unauthorized repair.

## Filing a claim
Ask Converse to open a warranty ticket with order number, SKU, and a fault description. Photos help for physical items.

## Assistant scope
The assistant can open cases but cannot approve claims. Expect email follow-up within two business days.
