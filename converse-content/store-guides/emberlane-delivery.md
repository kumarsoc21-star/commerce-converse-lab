# Ember Lane Delivery

## Processing
Physical orders (Apple, Samsung, accessories, smart-home hardware) ship from Ember Lane within **1–2 business days**. Digital gift cards and ebooks deliver by **email within minutes** — no shipping charge.

## Rates and speeds (continental US — physical goods)

| Method | Estimate after ship | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Standard | 3–5 business days | Free on orders **$50+**, else $5.99 |
| Expedited | 2 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight | Next business day (order by 1 PM ET) | $24.99 |

Large items such as bundled smart-home kits may ship in multiple boxes; tracking is provided for each.

## Where we ship
We ship physical goods to the continental US, Alaska, and Hawaii. Digital codes are emailed worldwide where permitted by the publisher or card issuer.

## Tracking
Use the link in your email or ask Converse for order status. The lab demo quotes this guide and can open a support ticket for detailed traces.
